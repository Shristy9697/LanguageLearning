Teaching Philosophy

The idea behind LanguageFeeling.

Most people memorize language.

I teach people to understand it.

For example:

“What's up?”

Points to something being "up".

“How are you?”

Points to something that is "going".

When learners understand meaning, grammar and vocabulary become easier to remember, easier to use and easier to transfer into real communication.
